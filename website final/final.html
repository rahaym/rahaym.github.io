This is the End (2013)

This review may contain spoilers.

Does it surprise anyone that the Directorial Debut of Seth Rogen is, once again, a movie with him and his mates smoking and drinking? Nobody?

There's nothing wrong with playing to type, and the movie is at least totally crazy. A returning Jay Baruchel wants to hang out with Seth, who drags him to a house party at James Franco's new hollywood mansion; right as the rapture begins and demons inhabit the earth. Note that no actors are raptured.

What follows is a funny, almost sketch comedy approach to surviving the apocalypse with greedy, self-obsessed idiots. It's a fun and different ride, but you get pretty much what it says on the tin: Stoner comedy with Seth and his mates.

Scene Stealer: Coked up asshole Michael Cera; Fire Axe Emma Watson.
